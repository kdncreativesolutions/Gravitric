Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 26, 2025

1. Overview

This policy explains how Gravitrik Digital (“we”, “our”, “us”) collects, uses, discloses, and protects personal information gathered through our website, marketing campaigns, lead forms, and CX operations.

2. Information we collect

  • Contact information such as name, email, phone number, job title, and company.
  • Marketing preferences, campaign briefs, and project requirements.
  • Analytics data such as IP address, device type, and browsing events using cookies or pixels.

3. How we use the information

  • Responding to inquiries, proposals, and service delivery.
  • Running Meta ads, social campaigns, and lead generation programs requested by you.
  • Improving our services, content performance, and customer experience.

4. Sharing and disclosure

We share information only with vetted service providers (hosting, analytics, calling support) under strict confidentiality and data processing agreements. We do not sell personal data.

5. Data retention & security

Data is stored in secure cloud infrastructures with access controls, encryption at rest, and monitoring. Information is retained only as long as needed for the engagement or legal requirements.

6. Your rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your data by emailing privacy@gravitrik.com. EU and UK residents can also object to processing or request portability as allowed by GDPR.

7. Updates

We may update this policy to reflect regulatory or operational changes. Any updates are posted on this page with a new “last updated” date.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email privacy@gravitrik.com or mail us at 600 Congress Ave, Austin, TX 78701.
